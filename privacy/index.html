The short version: this site collects nothing. The long version is also short.

// what this site does

This is a static website. It sets no cookies, runs no JavaScript, loads no third-party resources (fonts are self-hosted), uses no analytics and has no forms. There is nothing here that tracks you.

// hosting

The site is served by Cloudflare Pages (Cloudflare, Inc.). Like every web server, Cloudflare processes technical connection data (IP address, user agent, time of request) to deliver the page and to keep the service secure – legal basis: legitimate interest, Art. 6 (1)(f) GDPR. I have no access to and keep no copy of this data. See the Cloudflare privacy policy.

// email

If you email me, I get your address and whatever you write – that's the point of email. I use it to reply and for nothing else.

// your rights

Under the GDPR you have the right to access, rectification, erasure, restriction of processing, data portability and objection. Since this site stores nothing about you, there is usually nothing to exercise them on – but you can always write to matthias.leyendecker@proton.me, or complain to the supervisory authority, the Landesbeauftragter für den Datenschutz und die Informationsfreiheit Baden-Württemberg.
