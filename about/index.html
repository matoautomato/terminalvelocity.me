// about_

Fifteen years in fintech, mostly in payments, and I’ve seen the business from nearly every seat.

I started in sales, carrying a BNPL contract into the market at arvato (Bertelsmann) – which is where I learned what merchants actually buy, as opposed to what they’re sold. That pulled me into product.

As Head of Product at Paysafe Pay Later I ran a white-label BNPL platform for banks and merchants across regulated European markets, and took one from zero to live in twelve months. When Unzer acquired the business, I merged the platforms, the product orgs and the roadmaps without dropping merchants or volume on the floor.

Now I’m co-founder and CPO at Payla, where I own product, design and engineering, building BNPL infrastructure for the CCD2 and PSD3 era. Somewhere along the way the work stopped being purely product and turned technical too – I read the code, I ship side projects, and I’m a better product leader for it.

I work in English (and have since 2012), think in German, and count in Croatian and Serbian. I live near the Black Forest with my partner and our two kids. Off the clock it’s heavy or ambient music, photography, pen-and-paper RPGs, or reviving old computers.

Want the formal version? The CV is one click away – or just email me.
