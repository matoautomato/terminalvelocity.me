Fourteen years in payments, one domain, four vantage points. I sold BNPL at Bertelsmann, built it as Head of Product at Paysafe Pay Later, merged platforms at Unzer after the acquisition – and now I’m founding it: CPO & co-founder at Payla.

// the long way around

Most payments people know one seat at the table. I’ve sat in all of them – so I can usually tell you where a BNPL deal, build or integration will break before it does.

  1. 01 · sold it

    arvato Financial Solutions Bertelsmann

    Carried the BNPL contract into the market. Learned what merchants actually buy, what they think they’re buying, and the gap a product team inherits between the two.

  2. 02 · built it

    Paysafe Pay Later payolution · Head of Product

    Ran product for a white-label BNPL platform serving banks and merchants across Europe. Launched a fintech platform in twelve months. Regulated markets are a different sport – I learned the rules by playing.

  3. 03 · merged it

    Unzer post-merger platform integration

    Merged payment platforms, product orgs and roadmaps without dropping merchants or transaction volume on the floor. Integrations are where you learn that politics beats craft – and how to win anyway.

  4. 04 · founding it

    Payla CPO & Co-Founder

    BNPL infrastructure for the CCD2/PSD3 reality – compliant by design instead of retrofitted, and fast, because speed beats every other business metric.
